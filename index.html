The Unfiltered Blueprint

Quit the job.
Cancel the rent.
Buy the ticket.

Thinking of throwing your laptop in a lake and backpacking Southeast Asia, Australia, and New Zealand? Does looking at the eye-watering cost of rent in London make you genuinely angry that you weren't born into Victorian nobility? Is your mum currently telling you it’s a terrible idea and that you need to "focus on your career"?

If that sounds familiar, welcome home.

At 25, I did the math and realized a devastating truth: it was literally cheaper for me to quit my 9-to-5 and backpack through Asia than it was to rent a single bedroom in London while working full-time. So, that’s exactly what I did. I handed in my notice, packed a single bag, and spent 6 months chasing suns across Asia. To keep the dream alive, I set up base in Melbourne, worked for half a year, and used it to fund an epic loop around Australia and New Zealand.

I built this site to give you the unfiltered blueprint. Just a simple, country-by-country guide to surviving the budget-shocks, eating like a king, and preparing you for the greatest adventure of your life.
